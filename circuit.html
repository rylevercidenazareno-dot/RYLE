Circuit Design

Designing Electronic Circuits for Real-World Applications

What is Circuit Design?

Circuit design is the process of creating electronic circuits that perform specific functions using components such as resistors, capacitors, transistors, diodes, integrated circuits, and microcontrollers. It serves as the foundation of modern electronic systems and ensures that devices operate efficiently, safely, and reliably.

Circuit designers begin by analyzing the requirements of a project before developing schematic diagrams and selecting appropriate electronic components. Once the design is complete, simulations are performed to verify circuit behavior before building and testing a physical prototype.

Circuit design is widely used in consumer electronics, industrial automation, telecommunications, robotics, embedded systems, renewable energy, automotive electronics, and medical equipment.
